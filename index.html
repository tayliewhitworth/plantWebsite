Who we are

PlantLife is all about taking care of plants. No matter what type of plant you have, we know how to take care of it.
We believe plants provide three things:

Joy

Plants are living things that bring vibrancy and beauty to any space, making them a natural source of joy. Caring for plants gives people a sense of purpose and accomplishment. Research has shown that being around plants can reduce stress and improve mental health, leading to a greater sense of joy and well-being.

Growth

Engaging in gardening or other plant-related activities can provide a sense of purpose, accomplishment, and connection to nature, all of which contribute to personal growth and well-being. Plants are a rich source of nutrients, vitamins, and minerals that are essential for human growth and development, and form an integral part of a healthy diet.

Connection

Plants have the ability to bring people together by creating shared experiences and common interests, such as gardening or admiring the natural beauty of a flower. Many cultures have longstanding traditions and practices that involve plants, such as the use of herbs in traditional medicine or the exchange of flowers as gifts, which can serve as a means of connecting people to their heritage and community.

Houseplant Care Tips!

01

Water your plants!

Different plants have different water requirements, so it's important to research the specific needs of each of your plants. Generally, plants should be watered when the soil feels dry to the touch, but be careful not to overwater them as this can lead to root rot.

02

Provide Sunlight!

Most plants require a certain amount of sunlight to thrive, so it's important to place them in an area where they will receive the appropriate amount of light.

03

Monitor for pests!

Pests and diseases can quickly destroy a plant, so it's important to regularly inspect your plants for any signs of infestation or illness. If you notice any issues, take action immediately to prevent the problem from spreading.
